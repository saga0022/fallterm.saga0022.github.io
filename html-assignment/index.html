About Me

I am a Filipino born from the country of the Philippines on the 2nd of February 1997. I am the youngest of the three siblings in my family and we migrated here in Canada a couple of years ago. I love visiting musuems here in Ottawa especially the NGC and would love to visit more musuems soon. I love going out during fall season.

People who know me would describe me as a person who does lettering and art. I am an aspiring letterer and graphic designer and currently studying Interactive Media Design at Algonquin College. Making crafts, drawing letters and calligraphy is my passion that is why I love making handmade gifts to my loved ones. But on a side note, I love plants and watching movies.

Some of my hobbies are:

  • Calligraphy
  • Lettering
  • Painting
  • Growing plants
